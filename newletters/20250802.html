Changing Audio & Subtitle Defaults

Some releases have multiple audio tracks (different languages, commentary, etc). By default, Plex will always play the first track, which isn’t always English. Luckily, there’s a setting that allows you to choose your preferred audio (and subtitle) language, so it’ll always play in English, even if it’s not the first audio track.

  1. On Plex Web, go to Settings > Account
  2. Scroll down to Audio & Subtitle Settings
  3. Choose your preference for preferred audio language, subtitle language, and additional subtitle settings (if you like)
